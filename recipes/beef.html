Beef Recipe


Description

Treat yourself to the ultimate comfort food with our mouthwatering Roasted Beef! This recipe delivers tender, juicy beef with a perfectly seasoned crust that locks in all the natural flavors. Slow-roasted to perfection, the beef is marinated with a blend of herbs, garlic, and spices to create a rich and savory dish that melts in your mouth. Perfect for Sunday dinners, special occasions, or meal prepping for the week, this roasted beef pairs beautifully with roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a fresh green salad. Get ready to enjoy a classic and hearty meal that's sure to become a favorite!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef eye of round roast, at room temperature
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt, or more to taste
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder, or more to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. Make sure roast is at room temperature. Tie roast at 3-inch intervals with cotton twine; this helps it from drying out and allows it to cook more evenly. Place roast on a rack in a baking pan.
  4. Make sure roast is at room temperature. Tie roast at 3-inch intervals with cotton twine; this helps it from drying out and allows it to cook more evenly. Place roast on a rack in a baking pan.
  5. Place in the preheated oven and roast for 60 minutes, or 20 minutes per pound if your roast is larger or smaller. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 120 to 125 degrees F (49 to 52 degrees C).
  6. Remove from the oven and transfer to a cutting board. Cover loosely with foil, and let rest so juices can redistribute, 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Slice and serve.
